Examples of the < time > element

Date only(YYYY-MM-DD)

The folowing is a date: January 1, 2023

Time only(24-HOUR-Format)

The folowing is time: 13:45(1:45PM)

Date and Time combination

The folowing is date and time: January 1, 2023 at 13:45

Datetime with Time Zone

The folowing is datetime with a timezone: January 1, 2023 at 13:45UTC

Human readable Data with Datetime Atribute

The folowing is a human readable date: New Year's day, 2023
